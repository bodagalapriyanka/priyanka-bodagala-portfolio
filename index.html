About

Bridging clinical practice and data infrastructure

I'm a Clinical Data Analyst at UCSF and an AI/ML Product Engineer at Serapath Integrative, with a PharmD foundation and an MS in Health Informatics from the University of San Francisco. My work sits at the intersection of clinical workflows, healthcare data standards, and applied AI — building pipelines and models that clinicians can actually trust.

Before health informatics, I spent a year on hospital wards as a Clinical Pharmacist reviewing medications for 100+ inpatients per month. That bedside context shapes how I design data quality checks, FHIR mappings, and AI safeguards today — I know what a bad SNOMED code costs when it lands in a real chart.

My recent work spans QA pipelines for 2M+ DICOM radiation oncology records at UCSF, a production RAG chatbot on GCP Vertex AI, FHIR-compliant ETL across ICD-10 / SNOMED / LOINC, and analytical validation of digital biomarkers for Parkinson's and ALS clinical trials.
